Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it typically take to complete a website?

Our development timeline varies based on project scope. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality may require 6-12 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more specific timeline based on your requirements.

Do you provide web hosting services?

Yes, we offer web hosting solutions tailored to your website's needs. Our hosting packages include regular maintenance, security updates, and technical support to ensure your site remains secure and performs optimally.

What information do you need to start a custom software project?

To get started, we need to understand your business goals, target users, functionality requirements, and any technical constraints. Our discovery process will help identify these elements, but coming prepared with a general idea of your objectives will help streamline the initial consultation.

Do you offer maintenance and support after project completion?

Absolutely! We offer various support and maintenance packages to keep your website or software application running smoothly. These can include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and technical support for any issues that arise.
